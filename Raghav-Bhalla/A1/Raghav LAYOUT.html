Raghav Bhalla

// Flowers Are Forever

The Fish Bowl

To swim in my mystic abode, getting submerged in the beauty of cyclic repetition. As the wraped human body symbolises the cycle. Floral imagery subdues the elevated discomfort caused by rediscovery of self.



Dreamworks

As I dream I go through multiple visuals and not so coherent experiences. The journey being a rush of exhilirance. Flowers for me bind the experiences that I have and the dreams that I draw.



Jigsaw

Floral forms morphing the beauty of possesion that I aspire to have. The artwork can be visually distributed on a grid with an unordered placement of matter. A game I play with myself of trying to fit the pieces of my Jigsaw. The Muse

The coming of clouds to form puddles of reflection on the surface. A compilation of emotions such as satisfaction and relief.
