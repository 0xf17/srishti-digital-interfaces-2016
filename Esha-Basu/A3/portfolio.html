Shop of Dreams

Shop of dreams was a project collaborated with Creative Producer and Artistic Director: Symon Macintyre; where a group of dream catchers set up a real shop, that sell dreams. Something people would never expect to use currency to buy. We never have any contorl over dreams. People have all sorts of dreams: Whimsical, Fantastical, Scary, Realistic, etc. But how do we categorize these dreams into materials that can be sold in a shop. Can you at all buy dreams with money
