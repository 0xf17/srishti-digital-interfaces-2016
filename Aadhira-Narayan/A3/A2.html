Welcome

Art. The word in itself is a mystery. Practising it is a wonderful journey of unravelling the mystery behind. As Luis Jorges says, “The intention of the author is but a mistake—an infallible thing.” The story is read differently by each reader. A painting is interpreted uniquely by each individual. We must be more resourceful in order to be more sensitive to the needs of those

around us. We as designers, must put ourselves in the shoes of many. We have upon us the responsibility of changing our lenses and looking at things from various perspectives.


So here's a peek into my portfolio. The stories behind each piece, the hardships, the concept, the whole rhetoric.

May it move you, leave you in awe, disgust you or even put you in a complete fix. I hope for all of it, as long as it lets you add your own special perspective to it.
