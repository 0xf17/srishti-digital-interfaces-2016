Me

I am a Bangalore based graphic designer, illustrator, photographer and arts manager, studying at Srishti Institute of Art, Design and Technology, India. Not only is Visual Communication and Strategic Branding my main discipline but also my biggest aspiration is to become an art director/manager.I have an immense flair for management, deadlines and logistics.

On the sides, music and dance is for the soul, indeed! I am proud to say that I am a certified ballroom dance teacher in the following Latin-American dance forms: Jive, Samba, Rumba and Cha Cha. I also have three years’ lessons in Waltz, Tango and Slow Fox. A little bit of Salsa and Bachata, every now and then, is delightful too.

I also am a fine artist who enjoys being at leisure with a set of brushes and paints, be it oil or acrylic or water colour. I have a flair for the languages. You may even call me a ‘Grammar Nazi’. Apart from my love for English, I thoroughly enjoyed studying French in my later years of schooling and even aced it.

I have set my heart on travelling to the furthest ends of the globe and engaging with a multitude of cultures, lives and spaces.

I have a dream, a priceless one, my little vision of loveliness which is a five-year plan, to build a little home, in Bhor, a little city within the Pune district under the state of Maharashtra. This home will be part of a non-profit project dedicated towards the well-being and happiness of the unprivileged aged folk. All enquiries and thoughts are dearly welcome.
