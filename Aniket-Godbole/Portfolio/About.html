About Aniket Godbole

A student from Bombay living in Bangalore studying Art and Design. Grew up with a passion for exploring new places,travelling and technology.
Curiousity lead me to pickup a camera one day and go around clicking photos of things around my neighborhood, and there was no looking back after that.

Other than clicking photos I love cooking,making music with my guitar and practicing taekwondo.My goal is to travel around the world and document life as it exists. I also love stargazing and news about physics and computer technology.But amongst all these liking nothing can beat one liking which is my eternal deeep deep love for pizza!
