ABOUT THE ARTIST



Shraddha Rastogi, was born on 20th November 1996. She Went to DPS Noida since nur-12th.After which she joined Srishti Institute of Art, Design and Technology in Banglore.

She is interested in Photography. She wants to persue the same later on in life. She also likes to explore different kinds of work. She is a very adventurous person when it comes to travelling.

As a child I was exposed to photography by my dad. I was in 3rd class when I got my first camera. I was very excited to use it. I remember it was a camera which used batteries and reel. I had wasted a few photographs from one of the reels. My dad instead of scolding me gave me another reel.

He has always encouraged me to try new thiings and learn from my own mistakes.

Whenever we go for trips we both carry a camera each and compete with each other. He never really taught me anything yet encouraged me every day and helped me become much better.

She also likes to explore alot. For her this class was an experience to remember. As she was new to processing.
