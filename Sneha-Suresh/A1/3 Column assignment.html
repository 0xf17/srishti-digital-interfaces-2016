SNEHA SURESH

Visual Artist | Illustrator







Hi. I am a 20 year old visual artist born and brought up in Goa. I am currently studying in Srishti Institute of Art, Design and Technology, Bangalore. I enjoy working with brand identity,

illustrations and textiles. I have an affinity for vibrant colours and intricate patterns. My style ranges from illustrations to mixed media artwork. In October 2014, I took my first baby steps into the world of entrepreneurship when i founded Tucksac, an online store

offers ethnic roll-up stationery pouches, laptop sleeves and bags. In my opinion, 2 words that describe me best are passionate and ambitious.
